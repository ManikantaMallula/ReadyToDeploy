How to Use

Add Items with Nutritional Information:

Easily add the food items you consume throughout the day. Specify details such as proteins, carbohydrates, fats, and calories for each item.

Comprehensive Nutrition Tracking:

Track your daily nutritional intake with precision. Food Tracker allows you to monitor the essential components of your diet, including proteins, carbohydrates, fats, and overall caloric content.

Daily Status Tracking:

Stay informed about your nutritional progress by logging your food consumption every day. The application provides a clear overview of your daily status, helping you make informed decisions about your diet.

Personalized Dashboard:

Access a personalized dashboard that displays your daily, weekly, and monthly nutritional statistics. Visualize your progress and make adjustments to achieve your health and fitness goals.

User-Friendly Interface:

Food Tracker boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users of all levels to navigate and utilize its features. Whether you're a health enthusiast or just getting started on your wellness journey, Food Tracker is tailored to meet your needs.

How to Use:

Add Items:

  • Navigate to the "Add Items" section.
  • Input the food items you've consumed, along with their nutritional information.

Track Daily Status:

  • Visit the "Home" section to view your daily nutritional status.
  • Monitor your progress and make adjustments to your diet as needed.

Explore Insights:

  • Dive into the detailed insights provided on your personalized dashboard.
  • Analyze your nutritional patterns over time and make informed decisions about your dietary choices.
