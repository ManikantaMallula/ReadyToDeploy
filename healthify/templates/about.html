How to Use:

Add Items:

  • Navigate to the "Add Items" section.
  • Input the food items you've consumed, along with their nutritional information.

Track Daily Status:

  • Visit the "Home" section to view your daily nutritional status.
  • Monitor your progress and make adjustments to your diet as needed.

Explore Insights:

  • Dive into the detailed insights provided on your personalized dashboard.
  • Analyze your nutritional patterns over time and make informed decisions about your dietary choices.
